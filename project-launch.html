The Challenge

Job seekers often struggle with presenting their skills in a way that matches automated ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) while still appealing to human recruiters. QCV bridges this gap using advanced AI analysis to suggest improvements and format layouts perfectly.

"QCV doesn't just build a document; it builds your career story using data and intelligence."

Key Features

  • AI Content Generation: Automatically generate bullet points for experience based on job descriptions.
  • ATS Optimization: Real-time scan to ensure your resume passes through automated gatekeepers.
  • Professional Templates: 30+ clean, designer-made layouts that put your content first.

The Goal

Our goal with QCV is to reduce the average resume creation time from 4 hours to under 10 minutes, giving job seekers a competitive edge in today's fast-moving market.

Strategic Roadmap

In mid-2026, QCV will launch its 'Smart Matching' feature, which connects your verified resume directly with recruiters looking for your specific skill set.
